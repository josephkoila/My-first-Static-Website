Maasai Mara Spa Resort is the only resort in Kenya with a naturally heated pool. The resort is known as the healing place because of the therapeutic values associated with the spa and the the quite tranquility atmosphere of the wild life. Maasai Mara has been described in the past as the jewel of the Rift Valley and by the words of Bishop Hannington on the rst sight the most beautiful view in Africa. Set in the acacia surroundings of the oor of the Great Rift Valley, the Resort is 266 kilometers from Nairobi, Kenyas capital city, 114 kilometers from Nakuru town, 95 kilometers from the equator crossing and 20 kilometers from Narok town..
